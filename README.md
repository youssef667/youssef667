## About Me

Mechatronics Engineering graduate from Ain Shams University with hands-on experience in embedded software at **Valeo Egypt**, one of the top automotive suppliers in the region.

I work at the intersection of hardware and software — writing low-level drivers, integrating sensors, and building systems that have to be *correct*, not just functional. My graduation project was a fully autonomous scooter (Grade A+), involving sensor fusion, path planning, and real-time control.

Outside engineering, I'm a competitive chess player (~1800 rapid) and a competitive programmer on Codeforces.

`ROS` `Sensor Fusion` `Path Planning` `Computer Vision` `Embedded Control`

`C` `AUTOSAR` `Embedded`
